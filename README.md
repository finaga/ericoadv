# Erico Advogados — Market Research

A visual market research brief prepared ahead of the brand and website redesign for **Erico Advogados**, a boutique arbitration law firm based in Brasília, Brazil.

## What this is

A single-page HTML report covering:

1. What arbitration is — and why it matters for tech and business clients
2. Honest assessment of the current site at [erico.law](https://erico.law)
3. Competitive landscape — Brazilian competitors and global benchmarks
4. The market gap Erico can own
5. What premium boutique law firm design looks like in 2026
6. The Brasília visual opportunity (Oscar Niemeyer / modernist aesthetic)
7. Three positioning directions for the brand
8. Specific improvements over the current site

## Context

- **Client:** Erico Carvalho, founder of Erico Advogados
- **Location:** Brasília, DF, Brazil
- **Specialty:** Arbitragem (Arbitration), Superior Courts, Complex Commercial Disputes
- **Clients served:** Businesses, startups, government — including platform-scale tech companies
- **Languages:** Portuguese + English
- **Project phase:** Pre-design research and positioning strategy

## Stack

Plain HTML + CSS. No framework, no build step. Open `index.html` directly in a browser or deploy to any static host.
